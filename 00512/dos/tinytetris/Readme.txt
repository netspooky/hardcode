TinyTetris --- [512 bytes Game]
===============================

Introduction
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Only 471 bytes Tetris,
smallest in the world.
Made by Dwing in China.

System requirements
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
386+ CPU
DOS, Windows 9x/ME,
or DOSBox on Windows2000/XP.

How to play
~~~~~~~~~~~
Up    --- change angle
Left  --- move left
Right --- move right
Down  --- fall instantly
Space --- fall fast
ESC   --- exit
